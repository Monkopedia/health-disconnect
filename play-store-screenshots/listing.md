# Play Store Listing — Health Disconnect

## App Name
Health Disconnect

## Short Description (80 characters max)
View your Health Connect data in charts and widgets. Offline. Private. No ads.

## Full Description (4000 characters max)

Health Disconnect lets you explore your personal health data right on your device — no accounts, no internet, no data collection.

Connect to Android Health Connect and instantly visualize 35+ health metrics as interactive charts. Create custom views that combine multiple metrics, configure chart types, aggregation methods, and time windows, then pin them to your home screen as widgets.

YOUR DATA STAYS ON YOUR DEVICE
Health Disconnect never connects to the internet. It reads your Health Connect data, renders it locally, and never transmits anything. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no ads.

KEY FEATURES

• Browse 35+ Health Connect metrics — steps, heart rate, weight, sleep, blood pressure, nutrition, and more
• Interactive charts — line and bar charts with configurable aggregation (sum, average, min, max)
• Custom views — combine multiple metrics in a single chart and save for quick access
• Flexible time windows — view data by day, week, month, year, or all-time history
• Home screen widgets — pin any custom view as a resizable widget with configurable refresh intervals
• Export your data — save aggregated values or raw entries as CSV
• Share charts — export chart images in light or dark theme
• Advanced configuration — logarithmic and normalized Y-axes, data smoothing, bucket sizes, custom units, min/max labels
• Theme support — follows your system theme or choose light/dark manually

SUPPORTED HEALTH CONNECT DATA

Fitness & Activity: Steps, Distance, Speed, Exercise, Floors Climbed, Elevation Gained, Power, Wheelchair Pushes

Cardiovascular: Heart Rate, Resting Heart Rate, Heart Rate Variability, Blood Pressure, Oxygen Saturation, VO2 Max

Body: Weight, Height, Body Fat, Lean Body Mass, Bone Mass, Body Water Mass, Basal Metabolic Rate

Nutrition: Hydration, Nutrition, Active Calories Burned, Total Calories Burned

Other: Body Temperature, Basal Body Temperature, Respiratory Rate, Sleep, Menstruation, Cervical Mucus, Ovulation Test, Intermenstrual Bleeding, Sexual Activity

PRIVACY FIRST
Health Disconnect includes zero network permissions. Your health data is read from Health Connect, displayed on screen, and never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share.
